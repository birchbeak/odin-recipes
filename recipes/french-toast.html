French Toast Recipe

Description

Very standard french toast recipe. Good to know if you've never made french toast before. Simple and exquisite in its simplicity.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 6-8 pieces of bread(depending on size)
  • 1/3 a cup of milk

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs and milk in a bowl.
  2. Lubricate a flat pan with butter or some oil.
  3. Put the bread into the mixture then place in pan.
  4. Cook until properly cooked.
  5. Repeat with all bread until out of bread.
